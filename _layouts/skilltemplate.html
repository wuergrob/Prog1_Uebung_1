Weapon Type: Rifles
1 - 2-7 - - - -  10/8/4m Ammunition

Rifle-Attacks consume Ammunition, requiring you to reload when empty, which mutes, disarmes and slows you by 75% until your next turn. Basic attacks consume 1 Ammunition, skills have individual costs which are noted in their description.
Different rifles can have different ranges, which directly influences the ranges of all rifle-based skills.

The current and maximum amount of Ammunition can be checked in the AmmunitionBar Panel.

Mark interaction

Rifle based skills can have special interactions with Marked characters, these interactions are also detailed on the individual skills description.
